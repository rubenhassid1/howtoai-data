## Test your own ChatGPT

First, take a look at how much ChatGPT knows about you.

Prompt it this: "**Using your memory feature, who am I? As one-liners.**"

To start your ChatGPT over with me:

1. Go to **Settings** (top right, on your face).
2. Go to **Personalization** (menu on the left).
3. Activate your memory if you've never done it.
4. Start over with me by clicking on "**Manage memories**".
5. At the bottom, there is a red button "**Delete all memories**".
