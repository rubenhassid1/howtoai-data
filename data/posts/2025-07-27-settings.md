## One‑Shot Prompting

Having the right memory is the best way to start.

But you can also improve your prompt (after setting up your memory).

One-shot prompting is all you need to get better.
[Video]

A complete one‑shot prompt has three blocks:

1. Role & TaskTell ChatGPT who to be and what to do.2. Single ExampleShow one clear input–output pair.3. New RequestProvide the data you actually want processed.

**Prompt:**

> Role: Act as [expert persona].  Example input: [sample data]  Example output: [ideal answer]  Now process: [your real data]

---

## When Few‑Shot Beats One‑Shot

The rare cases when few-shot beats one-shot:
[Video]

**Rule of thumb**

• Zero‑shot → speed.
• One‑shot → clarity + single format.
• Few‑shot → varied formats, nuanced categories, or risk‑heavy tasks.
