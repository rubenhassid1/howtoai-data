## 1. How to find the best free AI guides.

The internet is (more or less) free. So education is free.

Learning AI is no different.

✦ You want to learn ChatGPT? Well, OpenAI has a free academy.

✦ You want to learn automation? Well, Zapier has a free academy.

✦ You want to prompt Gamma better? Well, Gamma made a prompt library.

If everything is accessible, why do you struggle finding it?

→ Because there is **too much information**.

So I made this list of the best-of free AI guides.

The goal is simple: let’s master the 20% that matters most.
