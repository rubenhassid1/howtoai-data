## How to access “nano-banana”

Three simple steps:

1. Go to Gemini.2. Make sure you select “2.5 Flash” (top left).3. Then click on the “tool” icon & “Create images”.

![](https://substack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com/public/images/0a737aef-966f-4617-bd68-1fd2ce1e7693_3456x2076.png)

**Why is it so good?**

It can generate images, sure. But ChatGPT & Midjourney are better.

But what “nano-banana” does better than any other AI: **editing images**.

Think Photoshop meets ChatGPT.

## My favorite image-editing prompts

A list of my favorite prompts.

**1 - Change your hairstyle.**

**Prompt**: “change my hairstyle to rock-long hair like I'm a rockstar”

![](https://substack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com/public/images/96118402-19a4-46bf-9e1a-7cb170d770f9_1254x1474.png)

**2 - Restore old photos.**

**Prompt**: “restore this old photo with today's realistic colors“

![](https://substack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com/public/images/6e88f05a-20f9-4f76-8ef9-bda58d2cfa38_1616x1540.png)

**3 - Replace X with Y.**

**Prompt**: “change X on right/left with Y”.

![](https://substack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com/public/images/1baf815f-6e18-45bb-bb4c-a8b52d6459e3_1174x1172.png)

**4 - Remove the background.**

**Prompt**: “Remove the background here”.

*PS: I still prefer Canva for this. Built-in tool is great.*

![](https://substack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com/public/images/194bbf53-5918-4e8d-b7c1-dc94aa8dbd3a_1194x1542.png)

**5 - Extract this from this picture.**

**Prompt**: “extract X, only the X”.

![](https://substack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com/public/images/b20816df-aaef-400d-a9dc-27b34565de7f_1614x1488.png)

**6 - Make a 3D model.**

**Prompt**: “Make [X] daytime & isometric. [x] only.”

![](https://substack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com/public/images/46854164-cd62-4ebb-94e2-3df37f78aafa_1140x648.png)

**7 - Change the color of anything.**

**Prompt**: “Change the color of [X] to [Y].”

![](https://substack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com/public/images/6f0caa5e-8038-4347-9901-4706161cb5ed_1502x1088.png)
