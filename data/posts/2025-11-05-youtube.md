## How to find the best videos to train your AI.

This is one of my best kept secret:

```
`starting from here, it’s a paid-member only guide`
```

You must know how to find the gems on YouTube.

#### The playlist hack.

1. Go to YouTube.2. Type the name of an authority on a topic.3. Select these filters:

![](https://substack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com/public/images/86f1887c-6203-43dc-af04-959d1a3904f2_2036x1688.png)

Then very quickly, you have access to entire playlists like this:

![](https://substack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com/public/images/938b01e6-13c8-48f6-b707-9f29165a447f_1060x1196.png)

Keep the videos for the second step. We will train an AI with them.

But first, let’s find other filters to find YouTube gems.

#### The duration hack.

Simply ask YouTube to show you “over 20 minute videos”.

![](https://substack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com/public/images/0606557e-d4f4-415a-b2eb-7ca82bd0fc9e_2302x896.png)

#### The all-out search.

I combine these filters:

- Subtitles/CC (if it has any, it’s usually good production).- Over 20 minutes (so it’s a masterclass).- [topic] + [authority]

![](https://substack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com/public/images/9183e2e8-6573-4851-bd58-58ee0669b261_2014x1650.png)

To find this:

![](https://substack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com/public/images/83e1ee94-b671-41c4-a471-ecb59efffe31_2068x764.png)

That’s it. The more you save good videos, the better your algorithm will be.

And that’s true with every single social media.

They are as good as you consume them.

→ Scroll and like trash? get trash.

→ Scroll and save gems? get new gems.

Now that we built ourselves libraries of awesome videos, let’s train our AI with it.

## The one AI to train (hint: it’s not ChatGPT).

NotebookLM (https://notebooklm.google.com) from Google.

It’s free, and has an unmatched Youtube integration. Especially for this use case.

#### Step 1. Create a new notebook.

I make one notebook per category.

For example: “Email copywriting by Alex Hormozi”.

#### Step 2. Extract the YouTube links.

That’s probably the hardest part.

You can upload up to 20 sources of information (so 20 YouTube videos).

Pick the best/longest/most recent ones.
