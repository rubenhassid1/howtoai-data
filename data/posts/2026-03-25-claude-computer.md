## 1. How to access ‘Claude Computer’.

The only way to access it:

1. Mac only (soon Windows, stay tuned).2. Download the desktop app claude.ai/download.3. Use a personal plan, not a business one (I guess it’s too much compliance).4. Upgrade your account to either Pro ($20) or Max ($100). It’s worth it.5. Go to your Settings (bottom left), and turn on these features:

> UPDATE: They now made Claude Computer available for teams.

![Click on your name (bottom left) and then on Settings.](https://substack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com/public/images/7808cc7c-8020-4369-aa36-89f2567fe6da_3456x2170.png)
*Click on your name (bottom left) and then on Settings.*

![Go to Desktop app > General > Turn on Browser use > Turn on Computer use.](https://substack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com/public/images/c9177d43-d022-4b56-b879-01400edb1e57_3456x2170.png)
*Go to Desktop app > General > Turn on Browser use > Turn on Computer use.*

![When toggling on Computer use, you must accept by clicking Turn on.](https://substack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com/public/images/e5313f86-aa06-4f54-82f1-f2922c6c52d8_3456x2170.png)
*When toggling on Computer use, you must accept by clicking Turn on.*

![You can deny apps. I denied my 1Password (it has my passwords).](https://substack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com/public/images/909c7523-5e4b-4398-ad6d-f0b9cb11c2fd_3456x2170.png)

## 2. My favorite use cases of ‘Claude Computer’.

A reminder on what’s needed to use Claude Computer, with your phone:

1. You must have the Claude desktop app + a paid personal account.2. Your computer must always be turned on. That’s why people buy Mac minis.3. Go to your phone. Open the Claude app. Go to Dispatch. Ask for something:

![My first prompt: go to my dashboard. Give me some data.](https://substack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com/public/images/a5df529b-e011-415a-802e-5687de02bfce_1206x2622.png)
*My first prompt: go to my dashboard. Give me some data.*

Now let’s do some harder tasks:

#### Find a freelancer for me.

Prompt:

```
`Go to my Fiverr account on Chrome. Post a job offer for [task]. Then, send a message to the best 10 [job needed] for [task]. Check if I have any unanswered DM, and follow up until one accepts the [task] for [budget] maximum.`
```

## 3. How to schedule tasks with Claude.

That was the obvious next step: scheduling a task with Claude.

Just like… an employee reporting to you.

1. Go to the left sidebar, and look for Scheduled.2. Create a New task. Give it a name and description.3. The most important part: the prompt. Test it before.4. Choose a frequency. And make sure to select the right model & folder.
