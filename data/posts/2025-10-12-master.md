## 1. How to find the gem through the noise.

Humans publish [6 to 8 billion](https://www.perplexity.ai/search/how-many-post-social-media-or-F7dKSn6yRYGaN1PdA0Zo_g#0) posts every single day on the internet.

So how could you know whom to feel inspired by?

### The wrong way…

✖  Pick the creator with the most views.

✖  Select the post with the most promising goal.

✖  Ask AI to find it for you (it will pick the most well-known).

### The right way…

Scrolling more (mindfully).

![](https://substack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com/public/images/68fd07b7-b012-4bfd-bfe8-8caef033b56a_675x499.png)

Your algorithm - would it be Linkedin, YouTube, X - is as good as your scrolling sessions. The first step of joining my team is to 1) create a fresh social account 2) type “AI” 3) like and save as many **good** (= useful) posts as possible.

Hide the rest.

Tada! You have a nearly perfect algorithm in about a week.

Now that you have found useful gems online, what should you do with them?

## 3. The exact steps to make one.

Let’s create your next mentor, together.

1 - Go to NotebookLM.

2 - Click on “**Create new notebook**”.

![](https://substack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com/public/images/e406646b-a7dd-493a-a195-28c56fac2b56_3456x1984.png)

3 - Add **sources** (PDF, YouTube, websites…).

![](https://substack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com/public/images/01d43b0c-93ed-4857-b861-8f6ca8d012e8_2528x1362.png)

4 - Eg. I want to create a notebook to sleep better. I love Andrew Huberman.

```
`**Andrew David Huberman** is an American **neuroscientist** and **podcaster**. He is an associate professor of neurobiology and ophthalmology at the Stanford University School of Medicine. He has been the host of the popular health and science-focused podcast Huberman Lab since 2021.`
```

So I will copy the links of all the YouTube videos from his channel that has the tag “sleep”. By the way, these videos are often over 3 hours.

![](https://substack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com/public/images/d7dd0df0-51eb-426e-a091-aafa6c44cb4d_2864x2040.png)

*Pro tip: Control + F “**sleep**” on his channel.*

I have now added 8 perfect sources around sleep, by Andrew Huberman and his guests. Time to ask anything!
