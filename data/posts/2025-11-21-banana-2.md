## How to access “nano-banana-pro”

Three simple steps:

1. Go to Gemini.2. Click on “Tools” + “Create images”.3. Or simply click at the top on “Try it”.

![](https://substack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com/public/images/19a036bc-1ba6-4a36-96a7-58d04c305745_3456x1990.png)

**Why is it so good?**

We call it SOTA = State-of-the-art = The best at [X].

From my first vibe check, Gemini is now SOTA on:

- Making the most realistic pictures.- Being the best for adding text to an image.- Being the best for editing images (like Photoshop).
