Google released “nano-banana-pro” right inside Gemini.

Here’s how to access it & my favorite case studies.

## **How to access “nano-banana-pro”**

Three simple steps:

1. Go to [Gemini](https://gemini.google.com/).
2. Click on “Tools” + “Create images”.
3. Or simply click at the top on “Try it”.

**4 - Add puppies (**from** **[Anisha Jain](https://open.substack.com/users/158407131-anisha-jain?utm_source=mentions)).

**Prompt**: “Add 16–20 bichon frises, bright white fluffy coats, small round ears, black noses. Distribute them along the sidewalk and curb at varying depths so it looks real. A few near the sitting bench, a few crossing the curb. Keep scale toy-size. Keep subject in a white outfit with sandals and white cloth unchanged. Crisp, editorial look. Negative/avoid: no gray/muddy fur, no clipping through curbs, no dogs in the room, no occlusion of the subject’s torso or face.”

*Just so you know, this case study went extremely [viral](https://x.com/jameygannon/status/1988647679367516462?s=20).*
