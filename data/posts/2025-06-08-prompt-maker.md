Is it the best prompt possible for such task? No.

Is it a fantastic start from a one-liner terrible prompt? The best you could have.

Now you only have to copy it & paste it on a new ChatGPT chat.

I made the Prompt Maker so it always creates a “Copy” button at the top right.

When copying & pasting, be careful with the green text: it says “Insert Linkedin post here” so it’s up to you to insert it the right way. To do so, I write this:
